Our Story

Our Roots.
Our Story.

The Barnes family has gathered across generations to celebrate love, legacy, and the bonds that no distance can break. Each reunion is a living thread in a story that began long before us.

In 2026, we come together again — to honor those who came before us, to celebrate each other, and to ensure the next generation knows exactly where they come from.

Whether you've been to every reunion or this is your very first — you belong here.
